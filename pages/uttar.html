Dashashwamedh Ghat

Dashashwamedh Ghat is a main ghat in Varanasi located on the Ganges River in Uttar Pradesh.
Fatehpur Sikri Fort

Built during the second half of the 16th century by the Emperor Akbar, Fatehpur Sikri (the City of Victory) was the capital of the Mughal Empire for only some 10 years
Taj Mahal

'Crown of the Palace' is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India.
Vrindavan

Vrindavan is a holy town in Uttar Pradesh, northern India. The Hindu deity Krishna is said to have spent his childhood here.
